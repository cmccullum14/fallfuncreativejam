I'll even be nice and share a quick go-to recipe for some amazing buffalo chicken dip if you want to have some while watching the big games.


CJ's Famous Buffalo Chicken Dip
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot® Original Cayenne Pepper Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup rance dressing
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumble (add a little handful more if you like a bit, like me!)
Directions
  1. PREHEAT oven to 350°. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.
  2. BAKE 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions, if desired, and serve with chips, crackers and/or cut up veggies.

Chicago Bears History

The Chicago Bears were first established on September 20th, 1919. They have won nine NFL Championships, and one Super Bowl. They even have more victories than any other NFL franchise.

The franchise was even founded in Decatur, Illinois. Who wouldn't want to follow the Bears?

They now play at Soldier Field in Chicago. The Bears has a long-standing rivalry with the Green Bay Packers (Boo!). They've gone through a very long reconstruction period. But now, they're better than ever before!

Why Da Bears
